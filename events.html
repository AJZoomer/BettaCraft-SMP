AJ Zoomer 100 Followers Special Event!

A special celebration stream for AJ Zoomer reaching 100 followers on Twitch!

Countdown to the Event

Event Details:

The BettaCraft community is coming together for a fun filled celebration as AJ Zoomer reaches the incredible milestone of 100 Twitch followers. Join us live on June 10th, 2026, starting at 7:30 PM AWST, for a chaotic, hilarious, and unforgettable night of challenges, surprises, and community fun.

The event will begin with AJ taking on a fiery challenge by trying Habanero hot sauce on a chicken tender. After that, AJ will be opening four packs of Japanese Pokémon cards live on stream, sharing the excitement with everyone watching.

Once the stream warms up, AJ will be joining voice chat to play VC games with viewers. To take part in the VC portion of the event, you must reach Level 5 in AJ Zoomer's Discord server, which grants the Regular Chatter role. You can join the server here: https://discord.gg/Yutu9v3qSz. This role is required to participate, so make sure to chat and level up before the event begins.

The celebration runs until 10:00 PM AWST, giving everyone plenty of time to join in, hang out, and enjoy the festivities. Whether you are there for the spice, the Pokémon pulls, or the community games, we look forward to seeing you as we celebrate this amazing milestone together.
