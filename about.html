About

BettaCraft SMP is a private, whitelisted Minecraft server for content creators. We are a long-running SMP, meaning we spend more than a year in the same world before moving on to a new one. Each season brings new ideas, new base themes and stories waiting to be told.

We have districts on BettaCraft SMP such as the Shopping district, the Gaming district, the Mining district and the Farming district.

We also have our own bases and in between everyone's bases and the different districts is connecting builds that connect everything together into one cohesive world for you all to enjoy.
